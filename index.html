Staff Roles and Promotion Paths

1. Helper

Responsibilities: Assist new members, monitor chat, participate in events.

Requirements: 1 month of active participation, helpful behavior, basic rule knowledge.

Privileges: Ability to issue warnings.

Next Step: Promotion to Staff.

2. Staff

Responsibilities: Support Helpers, handle minor infractions, help organize events.

Requirements: 1 month as Helper, good rule knowledge, minor dispute handling, recommendation from a Moderator or higher.

Privileges: Ability to mute members temporarily.

Next Step: Promotion to Head Staff.

3. Head Staff

Responsibilities: Oversee and support Staff, assist in organizing larger events, ensure Staff duties are performed effectively.

Requirements: 2 months as Staff, excellent performance, strong leadership skills, recommendation from a Supervisor or higher.

Privileges: Ability to issue more significant warnings and mutes.

Next Step: Promotion to Supervisor.

4. Supervisor

Responsibilities: Supervise all Helpers and Staff, handle more significant infractions, organize and lead events.

Requirements: 2 months as Head Staff, proven leadership, comprehensive rule knowledge, recommendation from a Moderator or higher.

Privileges: Ability to mute and kick members.

Next Step: Promotion to Moderator.

5. Moderator

Responsibilities: Enforce rules, resolve serious issues, mentor Helpers, Staff, and Head Staff, assist in large events.

Requirements: 2 months as Supervisor, strong conflict resolution, comprehensive rule knowledge, recommendation from Head Mod or another Moderator.

Privileges: Ability to mute and kick members, initiate temporary bans.

Next Step: Promotion to Head Mod.

6. Head Moderator

Responsibilities: Oversee all staff, develop policies, lead major projects, handle serious disputes.

Requirements: 6 months as Mod, exceptional leadership, proven project leadership, unanimous Admin approval.

Privileges: Ability to mute, kick, and ban members, high-level decision-making authority.

Next Step: Special appointment to Admin.

7. Admin (Highest Staff Role)

Responsibilities: Strategic direction, foster growth, mentor senior staff, executive decision-making.

Requirements: Appointment only, must have served as Head Mod, exceptional contributions, limited to three Admins.

Privileges: Full administrative rights, authority to appoint/remove staff.

Next Step: None, highest staff role.
